## Tech Debt & Forward-Looking Gaps

### User-Accepted Deferrals (not blocking milestone close)

1. **DOC-02 rollback exercise** — DR procedure documented but not exercised; recommended first exercise against low-risk `pbf` dataset in v1.6 or during first snapshot retention rotation.
2. **Live bootstrap execution** — Phase 32 Jobs not yet run; operator schedules per docs/BOOTSTRAP.md (~3.5h total on thor). WIRE-02/03 and full E2E complete after bootstrap runs.

### Latent Tech Debt (not in milestone scope)

1. **ArgoCD Applications not managed by parent App-of-Apps** — identified in Phase 29 CONTEXT. Manual edits drift without self-healing. Proposed for v1.6 hardening.
2. **CI guard preventing non-`main` `targetRevision` commits** — proposed in Phase 29 CONTEXT. Would prevent the kind of drift that caused Phase 29 to exist.
3. **civpulse-gis namespace pre-existed manually** (34d old at Phase 31 apply) — ArgoCD CreateNamespace=true was idempotent, but the namespace is now co-managed which could confuse future declarative bootstraps.
4. **No automated ZFS snapshot cron** — docs/ZFS-STORAGE.md documents the procedure; snapshot scheduling is operator-driven.
5. **No S3-archival of bootstrap PBFs** — DR currently assumes ZFS snapshot only; losing the ZFS pool requires re-bootstrapping from Geofabrik (external dependency).
